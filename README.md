  Key Insights
* The business generated $262.53M in revenue with a healthy 48% profit margin. 45% of deliveries are delayed, representing a critical operational challenge
* Kansas City is the busiest route with 8.7K trips
* Truck utilization rate of 77.50% is approaching the 80% industry benchmark
* First Group is the top revenue generating customer contributing nearly $9M
* Contract customers generate the highest revenue at $99M compared to Spot ($83M) and Dedicated ($81M)
